French Toast

Descripcion

This fabulous French toast recipe works with many types of bread â€” white, whole wheat, brioche, cinnamon-raisin, Italian, or French! Delicious served hot with butter and maple syrup.

Ingredientes

  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (Optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoo ground cinnamon (Optional)
  • salt to taste
  • 6 thick slices bread

Instrucciones

  1. Reunir todos los ingredientes
  2. Mezclar leche, huevos, vainilla, cinnamon y sal juntos en un bowl
  3. Calentar a media-alta temperatura
  4. Transferir a la hornalla caliente hasta que quede dorado, 3 o 4 minutos por lado
