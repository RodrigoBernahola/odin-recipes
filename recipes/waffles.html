Waffles

Descripcion

This waffle recipe is the only one you'll need to make homemade waffles with your waffle iron. Simple pantry ingredients mix up quickly in this easy batter that can be used right away or stored in the refrigerator for up to a week. Serve waffles hot with whipped cream and fresh fruit or with butter and maple syrup for either breakfast, brunch, or a snack.

Ingredientes

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Vanilla
  • Cooking spray

Pasos

  1. Paso 1

    Precalentar una olla de waffle de acuerdo a su manual de instrucciones.

  2. Paso2

    Romper 2 huevos en un bowl grande hasta que sea claro y vizcoso. Agregar harina, leche y aceite vegetal y revolver para combinarlo.

  3. Paso 3

    Dispersar la olla de waffle con un palo. Cocinar de 3 a 5 minutos.
